Hidden Gems of India

Discover Untouched Beauty

Beyond the famous landmarks, India hides countless unexplored destinations waiting to be discovered. These hidden gems offer peace, beauty, and authentic cultural experiences away from the crowds.

Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

A paradise for nature lovers, famous for its lush paddy fields, pine hills, and the Ziro Music Festival.

Majuli Island, Assam

World’s largest river island, known for its satras (monasteries), unique culture, and breathtaking sunsets.

Chopta, Uttarakhand

Called the "Mini Switzerland of India", Chopta is a base for treks with mesmerizing views of the Himalayas.

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

Home to the famous Tawang Monastery and spectacular mountain landscapes, rich in Buddhist culture.

Kanatal, Uttarakhand

A tiny, crowd-free Himalayan village perfect for camping under starry skies.

Gurez Valley, Kashmir

A breathtaking valley near the Line of Control, rich in natural beauty and cultural experiences.

Sandakphu, West Bengal

Offers stunning panoramic views of four of the world's five tallest peaks.

Patan & Modhera, Gujarat

Home to the stunning UNESCO-listed Rani ki Vav stepwell and the almost-forgotten Sun Temple of Modhera.
